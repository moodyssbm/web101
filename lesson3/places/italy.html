More Than Just the Boot

My family refers to this trip as the Italy trip, but in reality, it was a cruise where we visited many countries. A large majority of our trip was spent in the country of Italy, but we were able to spend a few days visitng a few other islands and countries.

Itinerary

We started in Rome before getting on the ship. Over the span of a week, the ship made various stops around Italy, Malta, and Croatia. Even though the ship had a restaurant open all day, my grandfather insisted that we try the local cuisine, and always made sure we had at least one meal at a local restaurant. During the last four days of our trip, my family stayed at a resort in Rome so we could explore the city and its cultural landmarks. Check out all the countries we were able to visit in just the span of one week:

  1. Italy
  2. Malta
  3. Croatia

Closing Thoughts

The cruise was a great experience. I would definitely recommend visiting any one of the countries I got to go to. The locals may speak a different language, but they were all friendly and willing to help an English-speaking tourist. The art was also phenomenal. Getting to see places like the Vatican were definitely life-changing. I loved every second of this trip.
